Case File: RMN - The Crimes of Reubenia

Subject: RMN (Reuben Mod Narcissus)

Alias: Reubenia

Age: 19

Nationality: Irish

Occupation: Irish Variety Streamer

Criminal Status: Under Investigation (Pending multiple charges)

Primary Allegations: Assault, Fraud, Destruction of Property, Cybercrimes, Environmental Terrorism

1. Kissing His Mods

Allegation: Assault (Unwanted Advances)

Details: Multiple moderators of Reubenia’s Twitch channel have come forward with claims that RMN engaged in unsolicited physical contact. During fan meetups, it is alleged that RMN kissed several mods without explicit consent, blurring the line between friendly behavior and personal boundaries.

Status: Under investigation. Statements have been collected from five separate moderators. More interviews are pending.

2. Touching People in Fall Guys

Allegation: Online Harassment & Game Tampering

Details: RMN was accused of repeatedly "grabbing" and "touching" other players’ avatars excessively in Fall Guys. Viewers and other players claimed this behavior crossed the line of good sportsmanship, targeting certain players to frustrate them.

Status: Evidence compiled through stream footage. No legal ramifications yet, though it has led to community complaints.

3. Internet Fraud

Allegation: Fraudulent Online Transactions

Details: RMN allegedly participated in fraudulent transactions involving cryptocurrencies and scammed his audience by selling fake products during streams and fake "giveaways" in exchange for money and personal information.

Status: Financial records are being analyzed. Victims are being contacted for further information.

4. Robbery

Allegation: Physical Theft

Details: RMN is suspected of involvement in a robbery of a local convenience store in Galway. CCTV footage shows a masked individual matching RMN's height and build, who allegedly stole €400 and several cases of energy drinks.

Status: CCTV footage is under review. Witnesses are being interviewed for identification.

5. Pyramid Scheme

Allegation: Fraudulent Business Practice

Details: RMN is accused of running a pyramid scheme targeting his Twitch audience. He promised streaming tips in exchange for investments, but those involved saw no returns, while RMN profited.

Status: Financial investigation ongoing. Multiple victims have come forward.

6. Purchasing Andrew Tate's Course

Allegation: Supporting Fraudulent Services

Details: RMN purchased Andrew Tate’s "Hustler’s University" course, a widely criticized scheme. While not illegal, the purchase raised concerns over his endorsement of unethical practices.

Status: Not a crime, but adds to the pattern of morally dubious decisions.

7. Purging His Mods

Allegation: Hostile Online Environment (Workplace Misconduct)

Details: RMN allegedly purged his mods, firing them abruptly when they expressed dissent. This led to claims of emotional abuse and retaliation against community members.

Status: Statements from affected mods have been collected, with further interviews pending.

8. Blowing Up France

Allegation: International Terrorism

Details: RMN allegedly participated in a coordinated online attack that disrupted French government websites and services. This extreme allegation is under serious investigation.

Status: International authorities are investigating, with evidence of RMN's involvement under review.

9. Flooding Galway

Allegation: Environmental Terrorism

Details: RMN is accused of tampering with Galway’s flood defenses during a storm, leading to catastrophic damage. There are reports he bragged about his involvement on Discord, but direct evidence is pending.

Status: Galway authorities are collaborating with environmental agencies to investigate the claims.

Conclusion

Reubenia’s case reflects a growing trend of influencers engaging in serious crimes and unethical practices. The severity of these accusations demands thorough investigation, especially in areas of fraud, robbery, and environmental terrorism.
